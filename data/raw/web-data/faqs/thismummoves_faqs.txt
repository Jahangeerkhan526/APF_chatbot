Q1. What is This Mum Moves?
This Mum Moves is an educational programme by the Active Pregnancy Foundation which aims to empower women to make informed physical activity choices throughout pregnancy and beyond by providing professionals with the tools to discuss physical activity during the childbearing years. The programme has been co-produced by women and professionals, piloted in five areas of England, and evaluated independently over the last three years. From 2022, the programme is being delivered in partnership by The Active Pregnancy Foundation and the Institute of Health Visiting (iHV).

Q2. What is the goal of This Mum Moves?
The goal is to improve the physical and mental wellbeing of pregnant women and new mums by reducing the decline in physical activity levels during and after pregnancy. Only 25 percent of pregnant women and new mums reach the recommended activity levels. With around 560,000 births in the UK every year, approximately 419,000 pregnant women are missing out on the benefits of being active.

Q3. Who delivers This Mum Moves?
This Mum Moves is delivered in partnership by The Active Pregnancy Foundation and the Institute of Health Visiting (iHV). It was originally launched in 2018 by ukactive together with several national partners, supported by National Lottery and Sport England funding.

Q4. What is the This Mum Moves Ambassador Training for professionals?
The This Mum Moves Ambassador Training is a half-day CPD accredited training event delivered live via Zoom. It equips professionals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to discuss physical activity during pregnancy and after childbirth. The training equates to 3 CPD points and provides access to a digital toolkit to support conversations and further learning.

Q5. How much does the This Mum Moves Ambassador Training cost?
The 4-hour individual training course costs 180 pounds per person with no VAT. The 4-hour commissioned group training course costs 3600 pounds with no VAT and can accommodate up to 24 participants. For queries email training@ihv.org.uk.

Q6. How do I become a This Mum Moves Ambassador?
There are three steps to becoming a This Mum Moves Ambassador. Step one is to complete the free Pregnancy and Postnatal Period Being Active e-learning module. Step two is to attend the CPD accredited This Mum Moves Ambassador Training workshop. Step three is to explore the resources on the Digital Toolkit and complete the Knowledge Checker assessment. As an ambassador you will be supported to raise awareness through social media and deliver the one-hour This Mum Moves Awareness Session to colleagues.

Q7. What do ambassadors get access to after training?
After completing the training, ambassadors get ongoing access to a digital toolkit which is a one-stop CPD access point for up-to-date information on raising awareness of physical activity. All Ambassador Training materials are available via the iHV Training Resources Hub. Ambassadors are also invited to join a private Facebook group to stay in touch with their cohort and continue the conversation.

Q8. Can organisations commission This Mum Moves training for their team?
Yes. Organisations can commission a bespoke training session for their group or team. Sessions are held online so team members can take part from anywhere in the UK. Up to 24 participants can join a commissioned session. The cost is 3600 pounds with no VAT. Contact training@ihv.org.uk for more information.

Q9. What free resources are available for professionals through This Mum Moves?
Professionals can access free downloadable resources including a Pregnancy Leaflet in print and digital versions, a Postnatal Leaflet in print and digital versions, This Mum Moves Posters, and This Mum Moves Brand Guidelines. These resources support professionals in promoting an active pregnancy and postnatal period.

Q10. What were the results of the This Mum Moves pilot programme?
During the three-year pilot, This Mum Moves trained over 400 healthcare professionals across five NHS pilot sites in England including Bexley, Cambridgeshire, Plymouth, Sheffield, and Sunderland. After training, 85 percent of healthcare professionals were committed or highly committed to applying what they had learned into practice. 81 percent felt confident to start a conversation about physical activity. 76 percent reported that they often or always provided physical activity advice. 97 percent said they would recommend the training to a friend or colleague.

Q11. Where can professionals find out more about This Mum Moves training?
Professionals can visit activepregnancyfoundation.org/thismummoves-professionals to find out about the CPD accredited Ambassador Training Programme, view booking information, and download the commission training brochure.

Q12. Where can pregnant and postnatal women find This Mum Moves resources?
Women can visit activepregnancyfoundation.org/thismummoves-mums for free Active at Home workout videos, free downloadable activity guides, and other resources to support an active pregnancy and postnatal period. Every trainer in the catalogue of free online workout videos is qualified to deliver their session.
