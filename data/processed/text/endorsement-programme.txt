ABOUT ENDORSEMENT
ee

Endorsement by the Active
Pregnancy Foundation (APF)
stands as a mark of distinction
for professionals delivering
physical activity programmes to
pregnant and postnatal women.
It reflects a steadfast
commitment to safe, engaging,
and effective workouts and
education, supported by the
necessary qualifications, valid
insurance, and alignment with
industry standards.

Endorsement is a journey and an opportunity for us to build a relationship with
you. Through mentoring, we will support you every step of the way and help you to
achieve these standards. We want you to succeed and hope that you will find the
endorsement process a positive and rewarding experience.

WHAT DO WE ENDORSE?
SCs

We endorse physical activity programmes, defined as follows:

e they have a start and end date and/or are delivered over a specific number of
sessions or within a specific period (e.g. Trimester 1);

they have clear objectives or outcomes;

they have a ‘sign up’ process;

they involve participant screening to determine suitability;

they involve pregnant or postnatal women (up to 12 months after childbirth);
they are delivered by qualified professionals holding valid insurance.

Programmes can be delivered online or in-person, take place as live or pre-
recorded sessions, and be delivered to groups or individuals. Examples include, a
pregnancy exercise class delivered at a leisure centre over 6 weeks; an online
pregnancy Pilates programme involving Trimester 1; or a community buggy
walking group for new mums.

Please note that we do not endorse individuals, single workout sessions,
businesses in their entirety, projects, products, or events.

WHY GET ENDORSED?
ee

Endorsement presents a
valuable opportunity to bolster
your credibility and underscore
your commitment to delivering a
safe and high-quality
programme. Securing
endorsement not only instills

confidence in women
participating in the programme
but also provides them and their
families with reassurance. During
a time marked by uncertainty
and change, endorsement
becomes a cornerstone in
establishing trust—an intangible
yet invaluable asset that should
not be underestimated.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS?
Lee

The APF Endorsement logo is a mark of quality. Not only will we promote your
programme on our website, but we'll also highlight your programme on our
‘Directory.’ This will set your programme apart from other activities in your
locality and those offered online.

You'll also:

e receive support & advice from an expert mentor in the field during the
application process

e have a dedicated ‘point of contact’ at the APF who can answer questions,
as well as give guidance & support after endorsement

e increase the potential for ‘sign ups’ and revenue

e be promoted through The APF website and social media channels

e be fast tracked for future partnerships & collaborations with The APF, for
example, a campaign or initiate.

NOTE: Your endorsement will be valid for three years. After this period, the
endorsement will automatically lapse, requiring renewal to ensure continued
alignment with APF requirements

ENDORSEMENT PROCESS

Register your interest HERE or on our website
This short form gives us some details about you
and your programme and will help us to ensure
that endorsement is right for you.

We'll arrange a discovery call

If endorsement seems like a good fit for your
programme, we'll arrange d call to talk through
the application process and discuss the fees
that will apply to you. If you decide to go
ahead, a mentor will be assigned to you and
the fee will become due. If endorsement isn’t
appropriate we will let you know why and you
will not need to pay anything.

Work with your mentor

Your mentor will assist you in preparing and
compiling the necessary evidence for your
application. This preparation will culminate in
an online presentation to the endorsement
panel, showcasing how your programme
meets the APF endorsement requirements.

Panel presentation

The panel will either approve, or make
recommendations for improvement, after your
presentation. If it’s the latter, you will be given
the opportunity to submit further evidence
within an agreed period, at no additional cost.

Ratification by Endorsement Committee

If approved, the decision will be ratified by the
endorsement committee and you will receive
your endorsement certificate and logo.

KEY REQUIREMENTS

Accredited

Cod

@O)OW

Qualified Professionals

Evidence that the professionals delivering on the
programme are appropriately qualified is required. Some
professionals may need to upskill to better understand
the guidelines in the United Kingdom.

Continued Professional Development
Proof of ongoing professional development, with a
minimum of 3 CPD points earned annually.

Valid Insurance

Proof of current professional liability insurance that
specifically covers working with pregnant and postnatal
women.

Professional Body Affiliation

We recommend that professionals be affiliated with a
recognised professional body, such as Chartered
Institute for the Management of Sport and Physical
Activity (CIMSPA).

Compliance with industry standards
Evidence of adherence to best practices and industry
standards.

NOTE: We'll be asking for details about the delivery of the programme e.g.
duration, format, etc., but we’re NOT looking to assess the content i.e. we will not
need to watch a class or session.
